As a designer,

I swear to fulfill, to the best of my ability and judgment, this covenant:

I will respect the freedom of all human-beings, I will not force, deceive, nor lure users to take actions they are not intended to take.

I will remember that I as a designer, care about the users as ends themselves, users experience as users’ experience. Benefits might come along the way, but I should never aim for them.

I will keep in mind that even when I’m design for a tiny little problem, my decision will affect populations beyond just a small group. My responsibility includes their families, social groups, communities, and even cities, countries and the world. There is no small problems, and I will always be aware of the consequences of my choice.

Activity

Code of Ethics' Tarot of Answers

Think about your problem and chose a tarot with your instinct...

“What would a great designer do?” Do you have a designer role model? What would he/she do in the situation? Do the things that every great designer should do.

“What brings greater happiness to the world?” Value all the possible good and bad concequences, and find a balanced plan.

“Who will be affected by my choice?” List all the possible stateholders. Are you rescpect and consider all of them?

“Good choices come from good intentions” Why are you making the desicions you are making? Are these intentions good?
